Who I am

I am a senior certified IT/OT engineer specialized in the OSIsoft PI System. I started at OSIsoft in the Paris office in 2014, initially in technical support, and later diversified by providing training for PI system users. From 2018 to 2022, I was assisting in supervising technical support in EMEA and have been part of the visualization tools escalation team, working closely with PI Vision developers in particular. I also handle the programming of internal applications (c#, html/css, angular, typescript).

In 2022, following an enriching 8-year tenure at OSIsoft, I made the decision to embark on a freelance journey. Drawing upon my extensive expertise, I now specialize in helping companies to navigate and embrace the transformative power of data.
